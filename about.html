Meet the Founders

Behind every meaningful change is people bold enough to imagine it. Meet the visionaries whose drive and dedication continue to shape our mission and inspire our community.

Founded in 2019

Potential is widespread, but opportunity is not. Across South Africa, young people are ready to step into the digital economy but systemic barriers stand in the way.

iHub exists to bridge that gap. We're digital disruptors focused on future skills for African youth, combining market-relevant technical training in UI, web development, and emerging tech with the meta skills and career-building support that turn potential into employment.

Our Mission

Is to create a meaningful, career-accelerating environment for young African digital talent by identifying and nurturing the right skills for the digital economy.

Our Vision

Is to provide immediate access to opportunities that will unlock the potential of a new generation, ultimately connecting them to the local & global digital economy.

The iHub Ecosystem

At iHub Africa, we believe transformation doesn't happen in isolation. Our ecosystem connects learning, work, and impact.

The Academy

Where potential takes root.

Our academy is the foundation. Here, young people gain market-aligned skills, and grow in confidence, mindset, and readiness.

[ihda] agency

Bringing together impact & sustainability.

Our in-house digital agency bridges the gap between learning and real-world experience. It balances delivery of high-quality client work with structured experience for graduates.

The Alumni Network

Where growth comes full circle.

Our alumni network keeps the momentum going long after graduation. It exists to provide ongoing professional development, mentorship, and opportunities.

Partnerships

Where impact multiplies.

We partner with organizations that share our belief in youth-driven change. Funders, collaborators, and industry partners create pathways for real opportunities.

Our ecosystem exists to make youth development sustainable. When students thrive, the agency grows. When the agency grows, it funds more opportunities. When alumni and partners reinvest, the cycle strengthens. It's a system built on shared value, turning potential into purpose, and purpose into lasting life-changing impact.
